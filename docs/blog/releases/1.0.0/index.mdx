We are thrilled to announce the official release of **Pika v1.0.0**! 🎉

Pika is our brand new, open-source PWA (Proactive Web App) platform designed to make web notifications, engagement, and user communication easier than ever. After months of development, testing, and feedback from our early adopters, we’re excited to share Pika with the world.

<!-- truncate -->

## 🚀 What is Pika?

Pika is a modern notification and engagement platform for web applications. It enables you to send targeted, real-time notifications to your users, track delivery and engagement, and manage your communication strategy—all from a beautiful, intuitive dashboard.

## ✨ Key Features

- **Easy Integration:** Add Pika to your web app in minutes with our simple SDK.
- **Real-Time Notifications:** Instantly reach your users with push notifications, in-app messages, and more.
- **Advanced Targeting:** Segment your audience and send personalized messages based on user behavior.
- **Analytics & Insights:** Track delivery rates, open rates, and user engagement with detailed analytics.
- **PWA Ready:** Built from the ground up to work seamlessly with Progressive Web Apps.
